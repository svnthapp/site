Multimedia

Take your pick of music input. Synthery works well with MIDI instruments, manual keyboard presses, or just a BPM input.

Customizable

Create unique visual performances. Personalize your choice of visuals and have them match with your music, regardless of genre.

Effortless

Visuals are triggered in real-time. Simply save your presets and connect to Synthery for great visuals when you next perform.

  • #1 Map your visuals

    Select your choice of device input, then map each key of your device to a corresponding graphic of choice. Visuals will be triggered in synchronization with the music for the MIDI musician & manual performer, but out-of-sync for the BPM user.

    Find out more about Synthery

  • #2 Customize your performance

    Personalize your visual performance by adjusting variables like colour, speed and size of the visuals. For more creative rendering, set your own scenes, rearrange or remap your visuals and best fit your tunes.

    Find out more about Synthery

  • #3 Play as always

    Now that we've got everything else out of the way, simply hit save and you're well on your way to a great performance. Continue to play your music as always, for we've got you covered on the visual side.
